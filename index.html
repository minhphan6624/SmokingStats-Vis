Is Vaping the New Smoking?

Exploring Trends in Tobacco Consumption

Smoking, both traditional and vaping, has become a widespread habit globally, affecting millions of individuals. While the use of tobacco is declining the regular use of e-cigarette products is increasing in popularity (OECD, 2023). This increasing prevalence of e-cigarettes in recent years has sparked debates and concerns regarding their impact on health.

Additionally, the consumption patterns of tobacco and vaping products vary significantly across different countries and over time, influenced by cultural, economic, and regulatory factors.

These changes globally have been visualised below. Through the three datasets the Percentage of Population who are Daily Tobacco Smokers and Users of Vaping products can be seen on the map. This is futher detailed through the chart to the right which displays these trends together and the number of people per country. The Cigarettes Per Person per day is also visualised on the map across countries and the trend can be viewed per country with the accompanying line chart.
